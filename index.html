Australian Airports

Visualising the distribution of airports accross the country.
This graph shows the size and elevation of all airports ranging from small to extra-large.
Size is based upon the geographical size of the airport ie. its footprint, rather than total visitors.
Geographic data was sourced from: https://www.abs.gov.au/AUSSTATS/abs@.nsf/DetailsPage/1270.0.55.001July%202016?OpenDocument
Airport information was sourced from: https://data.world/ourairports/6fee2ce0-5e8e-4138-a794-f5e9052f38c6
